The obstacles in Flappy Bird are really just rectangles that look like pipes. Use the rectangle tool.

![](https://guides.codingpirates.dk/rails/active_storage/blobs/redirect/eyJfcmFpbHMiOnsibWVzc2FnZSI6IkJBaHBBcFlDIiwiZXhwIjpudWxsLCJwdXIiOiJibG9iX2lkIn19--50521d3154953e65689a72301057a8320d6b4238/Screenshot%202025-09-05%20200156.png)
